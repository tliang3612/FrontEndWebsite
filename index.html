Welcome to Phu's Bakery!

Phu's Bakery is a small bakery located in Rosemead, CA. We specialize in Asian pastries and desserts.

Popular Items

  • Egg Tart

    Egg tarts come in different shapes and sizes, but the most common type is the Portuguese egg tart. It has a crispy and flaky crust, with a slightly burnt and caramelized top that gives it a unique flavor. The filling is usually made with eggs, sugar, cream, and vanilla, and is baked at a high temperature until it sets.

  • Mooncake

    Mooncakes have a flaky crust that is made from wheat flour, sugar, and oil. The filling is usually sweet and dense, made from a mixture of lotus seed paste, sweet bean paste, or sometimes salted egg yolks. Some modern varieties also incorporate flavors like green tea, black sesame, or even ice cream.

  • Taro Bread

    The bread is made by mixing taro puree into the dough, which gives it a soft, fluffy texture and a slightly sweet taste. Taro bread often has a light purple hue, and is usually dusted with flour or sesame seeds on top for added texture and flavor. It is also commonly used as a base for sandwiches or toast.

  • Fruit Cake

    Fruit cake is made with a combination of flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and a fruit of your choice. Chunks of fresh or canned mango are added to the batter, which gives the cake a sweet and tangy flavor and a moist texture. The exterior of the cake is also decorated in mango slices for a unique look and extra taste.

About Us

Phu's Bakery is a small, family-owned bakery that has been serving up delectable Asian pastries, bread, and desserts since 2014. With a commitment to using only the freshest ingredients, Phu's Bakery has become a go-to destination for those seeking an authentic experience.

At Phu's Bakery, we take pride in our extensive selection of mouth-watering baked goods. Our menu features a variety of Asian-inspired pastries, including egg tarts, bao buns, and mooncakes, as well as a wide array of bread and desserts. Our skilled bakers use only the highest quality ingredients, ensuring that each and every item that comes out of our ovens is of the utmost quality and taste.

We are passionate about providing our customers with the best possible baked goods and customer service, and we take great pride in being a part of the community. Whether you are a long-time customer or a first-time visitor, we look forward to sharing our delicious baked goods with you.
